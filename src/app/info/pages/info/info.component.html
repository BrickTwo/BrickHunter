If you like BrickHunter I would appreciate a voluntary donation.

BrickHunter is a non LEGO authorized browser extension that assists the user with a Pick a Brick or Bricks & Pieces order. No liability is assumed for the execution of the order, this applies in particular to the correct finding and filling of the shopping cart of a part in shape, color, price or quantity. The user is requested to check the order before sending it to the LEGO Shop.

BrickHunter is programmed to minimize the amount of automatic background interaction with LEGO. BrickHunter is not liable for possible errors or blockages caused by BrickHunter.

BrickHunter is developed under the GPL-3.0 License.

Source code available on GitHub.

 If available for your country, orders placed with BrickHunter will be processed with an affiliate link. There are no additional costs for you, but you support that BrickHunter can still be offered in this form. However, you can switch off the use of an affiliate link before you hand it over to LEGO.
